# About Origami Trail

A side-scrolling shooting gallery where origami creatures fly across the screen in waves of increasing difficulty.

## How to Play

Tap or click to shoot origami creatures as they move across the screen. Each wave brings faster creatures and trickier patterns. Miss too many and the round ends.

## Scoring

Every hit scores points. Chain hits together for combo multipliers — the longer your streak, the bigger the bonus. Time bonuses reward quick reactions.

## Creatures

The trail is populated with folded paper creatures — cranes, frogs, butterflies, and more. Each has its own flight pattern and speed. Some dart across quickly, others float lazily but are worth fewer points.

## Modes

- **Solo** — Shoot for a high score across escalating waves. See how far you can get.
- **Crew** — Compete with your crew on the same set of waves. Highest score wins the round.
