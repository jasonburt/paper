# About Paper

Paper is a collection of origami-themed mini games. Throw paper airplanes, shoot origami creatures, and compete with friends.

## The Games

**Toss Paper** — A paper airplane throwing competition. Drag to launch, watch the physics play out, then place an obstacle for the next player. Wind, gravity, and obstacles make each throw unique.

[About Toss Paper](/about/toss-paper)

**Origami Trail** — A side-scrolling shooting gallery inspired by classic arcade games. Shoot origami creatures across waves of increasing difficulty with combo multipliers and time bonuses.

[About Origami Trail](/about/origami-trail)

## Paper Crew

Create a crew, share the 6-character invite code, and compete on shared leaderboards. Players take turns throwing and placing obstacles that persist for everyone. Both games support solo practice and crew multiplayer.

## Design

The aesthetic is clean — like a white piece of paper. Color comes from the origami itself. Star Yellow, Petal Orange, Gift Red, and Sky Blue bring the folded paper to life against a minimal white backdrop.
