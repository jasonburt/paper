# About Toss Paper

A paper airplane throwing competition where physics, wind, and player-placed obstacles make every throw different.

## How to Play

Drag backward on your paper airplane to set the launch angle and power, then release to throw. The airplane follows realistic physics — wind gusts push it off course, gravity pulls it down, and obstacles block its path.

## Scoring

Distance is everything. The farther your airplane flies before hitting the ground or an obstacle, the higher your score.

## Obstacles

After each throw, you place one obstacle on the field. In crew mode, your obstacle stays for every player who throws after you. Strategy matters — place obstacles to protect your lead or block the paths others are likely to take.

## Modes

- **Solo** — Practice throws against your own best distance. No obstacles, just you and the wind.
- **Crew** — Take turns with your crew. Each player throws, then places an obstacle. Compete on the shared leaderboard.
